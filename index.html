“I build the team and the design system when neither exists yet.”

Over 7 years in product design, I’ve gone from building products with my own hands — apps, websites, full brand identities — to building and leading the teams behind them.

As a lead, I own the full management cycle — hiring, onboarding, performance, and team growth from a single designer to teams of 10. In parallel, I build and maintain design systems as products in their own right: unifying fragmented interfaces into one cohesive language and cutting development time in half.

I’m just as comfortable presenting ROI to C-level stakeholders as I am deep in Figma. And lately, I’m rebuilding how design itself gets done — embedding AI into routine tasks, and in some cases replacing the classic process entirely, working through Claude Code and Git without Figma in the loop.
