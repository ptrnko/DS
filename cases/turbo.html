Project Overview

Turbo.ua —
grocery delivery
in 30 minutes.

A DoorDash-style grocery delivery service for Kyiv: dark stores in every district, plus a courier app with precise delivery addresses, customer comments, and real-time GPS order tracking.

Role
Lead Product Designer
Platform
iOS · Android · Web
Sector
Delivery · B2C

Main Goals

30-min delivery, zero hassle. No stock-outs. No delays. Automated shelf management.

01

Create a service as easy to use as possible while guaranteeing high customer orientation.

02

Avoid out-of-stock situations and courier delays — learn from competitor weaknesses.

03

The system puts everything on shelves automatically — both money and goods.

User pain points

Availability

Products out of stock?

Fear that not all products will be in the order. Something missing to prepare a meal.

Confidence

Speed

Will it arrive in time?

Fears that the courier will take too long and perishables will deteriorate.

Quality

Is quality guaranteed?

Worried about quality of goods and products ordered online vs in store.
