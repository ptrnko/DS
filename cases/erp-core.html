ERP & CRM · B2B

Optimizing ERP core.
From fragmented workflows
to a unified matrix

Role Lead Product Designer
Timeline 6 months
Scope ERP / B2B SaaS

“We were forced to keep the real data in Excel and paper notes because the system wouldn’t let us see the full picture on one screen.”

Distribution manager, Wombat Digital

“75% of product distribution errors traced back to one place: data entry and reconciliation. The system meant to prevent mistakes was silently creating them.”

Operations lead

Give distribution managers full control over data — so errors get caught before they become financial losses, not after.

As discovered

Poor Discoverability

Distribution managers using the system had no idea the workflow features actually existed. Left-column navigation as a concept is fragmented and unfit.

Steep Onboarding

But even with distribution managers directly to inform their day-to-day workflow and answer the system forgot Layout: it.

Usability Pitfalls

Tested the existing interface flow to confirm what the data shows. Then studied early static prototypes, then confirmed the same results.

The solution product detail

Intelligent Matrix Distribution Interface

The Matrix allows real-time look at both Orders Demanded falls into Demands tables. This is a two-pane spreadsheet that lets you immediately verify and confirm all data in one table domain.
