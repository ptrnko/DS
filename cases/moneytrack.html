Project Overview

Money Track —
personal finance
made simple.

A personal finance management app with bank card sync, automated spending tracking via QR receipt scanning, and built-in financial literacy courses — designed for users who want control without complexity.

Problems & Solution

Real problems, thoughtful answers.

The research revealed two core user frustrations. Here's how Money Track addresses each one directly.

Problem 01

Lack of financial literacy

Users don't have enough knowledge to manage their finances effectively and feel overwhelmed by complex tools.

Solution 01

Built-in tutorial section

Money Track includes a tutorial section with lessons of varying difficulty. Users can choose the "beginner" level and listen to only the basic lessons.

Problem 02

Not being able to plan your budget

Users lack visibility into where their money goes and struggle to set meaningful budget goals.

Solution 02

Link your card. Track everything.

The app offers to link your card to track and segment spending. You can also scan the QR code from a receipt — the operation is added automatically.

Problem 03

Lack of time to learn new tools

Existing budget planning apps are feature-heavy and demand a steep learning curve most users never have time for.

Solution 03

Minimal features. Maximum clarity.

Simple interface with a minimum number of features. Financial literacy courses are also available depending on the level of the user.
